About Bottlebits

Committed to bringing the most unique experiences for whisky enthusiasts, collectors, and connoisseurs, our professionals come from diverse backgrounds including technology, fine spirits, luxury, management, and finance.

Dramification

Our unique structure of acquiring and collecting whisky that allows people around the world the opportunity to buy ‘’Drams’’ of rare and sought-after bottles of whisky. These bottles tend to become more valuable over time, so buying a dram now to enjoy later, just makes sense.

How it works

01 Bottlebits is acquired

We have full ownership and possession of the bottles, they are insured and stored in a specialised warehouse.

02 Explore

Go through our unique bar of bottles.

03 Buy Online

Buy a dram or a whole bottle of your choice.

04 Experience

Explore your bottle, share it, grow your collection, and keep learning. Access cool digital and in-person experiences.

05 Exit

Sell-back your drams through our platform, collect all and exchange them for the bottle, or wait for Bottlebits to launch the "Drink the Dram" experience.
