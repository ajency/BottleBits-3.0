How it all started...

Inspired by a passion for tradition, born as a tribute to a queen, crafted with a desire for perfection.

Few men have built their own distillery with their own bare hands. But that’s exactly how William Grant started writing our story

For 20 years William Grant nurtured a dream to make the ‘best dram in the valley’. With the help of his family, he finally achieved that vision.

In the summer of 1886, with his seven sons and two daughters by his side, William set out to fulfil a lifelong ambition. Together they began building his Distillery by hand, stone by stone. After a single year of work it was ready and William named it Bottlebits, Gaelic for Valley of the Deer.

Over the decades, Bottlebits master blenders have collected and stored whiskies of immense distinction. Behind locked doors, these liquids lie in wooden casks, ageing, maturing, patiently waiting for the moment they are ready to make magic.
